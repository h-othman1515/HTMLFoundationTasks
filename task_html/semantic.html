Getting Started with Web Development

Web development is an exciting journey that combines creativity with technical skills. Whether you're interested in frontend design or backend programming, there are endless opportunities to learn and grow. Starting with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript gives you a solid foundation to build amazing websites and applications.

The Importance of Accessibility in Web Design

Creating accessible websites ensures that everyone, including people with disabilities, can navigate and use your site effectively. By using semantic HTML, proper alt text for images, and keyboard navigation, you make your content available to a wider audience. Accessibility isn't just a feature—it's a responsibility we all share as web developers.
